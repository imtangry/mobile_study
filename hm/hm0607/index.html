Welcome To New York is a 2018 Indian 3D comedy film, directed by Chakri Toleti.
  Welcome to NewYork!  

Teji (Diljit Dosanjh), a sloppy recovery agent who dreams of being an actor, and Jeenal Patel (Sonakshi Sinha), a fashion designer, become part of a big Bollywood event in New York - and chaos ensues.[2]
